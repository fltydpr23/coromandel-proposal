Why It Matters
Four structural changes.
01
Credibility Before Exploration
In the original flow, the visitor encounters the map and logo ticker at positions 2–3. By the time they see your client roster, they've been asked to explore without a reason to trust. Moving the ticker and showcase to positions 2–3 means the visitor forms their first judgment of scale and legitimacy before they've scrolled past the fold.
Trust at position 8 → Trust at position 2
02
The Founder Arrives as a Reveal
Placing the Founder section at position 6 transforms it from an introduction into a reward. The visitor has already been sold on what Coromandel does. The Founder's story now answers the question they're already asking: who built this?
Introduction → Reveal
03
The Map Earns Its Scale
An interactive global reach map is a bold claim. Appearing second makes it an assertion they must accept on faith. Appearing at position 7, after clients and services, it functions as confirmation of what they've already come to believe.
Claim (position 2) → Confirmation (position 7)
04
Action as Natural Conclusion
The "See All Our Content" section functions as a soft conversion point. Moved to the final position, it becomes the natural closing gesture: everything built to full conviction, one clear invitation to go deeper.
Interrupts the funnel → Closes the funnel
