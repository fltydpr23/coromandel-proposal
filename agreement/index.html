Project Overview
A custom cinematic website built to elevate Coromandel's global positioning.

Lune Studio will design and develop a custom cinematic website for Coromandel Productions. The website is intended to elevate Coromandel's global positioning and serve as its primary digital presence.

The project includes strategy, design, development, content structuring, CMS setup, optimisation, QA, and launch support.

04
Governance & Revisions

How We Work Together

Working Relationship
Both parties agree to communicate in good faith and make reasonable efforts to maintain project momentum throughout the engagement. The success of the project depends upon timely collaboration, feedback, and decision-making from both parties.
Approvals
Feedback and approvals provided by the Client shall be considered final for completed phases. Requests to revisit previously approved work shall be treated as additional scope and billed accordingly.
Change Requests
Any request that materially alters the approved scope, information architecture, functionality, integrations, content structure, or design direction shall constitute a Change Request. Change Requests may require revised timelines and additional fees.
Revision Policy
Two structured revision rounds are included per major project milestone. Revision requests must remain within the approved scope of work. Requests involving new functionality, integrations, information architecture changes, content restructuring, or significant design direction changes shall be treated as additional scope.

Additional scope may require revised timelines and fees.
2 Revision Rounds Included
Acceptance of Deliverables
Deliverables shall be deemed accepted if no material issues are reported within five (5) business days of presentation.
Client Responsibilities

The Client agrees to provide all required materials in a timely manner to maintain project momentum.

Important Note for Coromandel
Delays in the delivery of required assets, approvals, or content may impact project timelines and delivery schedules.
  • Brand logos and assets (high-resolution files)
  • Video content and specifications
  • Photography and imagery
  • Client logos and partner assets
  • Copy, text content, and messaging
  • Access credentials or platform accounts
  • Consolidated feedback within five business days
Project Pause
If the project is paused by the Client for more than 14 days, the project timeline may be adjusted and the project may be rescheduled based on Lune Studio's availability.
Cancellation
In the event of project cancellation, the advance payment remains non-refundable. Completed work and deliverables shall be invoiced proportionally based on work completed at the time of cancellation.

06
Liability & Confidentiality

Scope of Responsibility

Limitation of Liability
Lune Studio's total liability under this agreement shall not exceed the total fees paid under this engagement. While Lune Studio commits to professional execution and best practices, the Studio cannot guarantee or assume responsibility for:
  • SEO rankings or organic search performance
  • Protection against third-party service outages or platform failures
  • Failures, outages, or limitations of hosting providers or external services

The Client is responsible for maintaining secure passwords, access credentials, and ongoing website maintenance after launch.

Mutual Confidentiality
Both parties agree to maintain confidentiality of all proprietary information shared during this engagement. This includes, but is not limited to:
  • Business strategies and commercial information
  • Unreleased content and creative direction
  • Financial data and pricing structures
  • Internal communications and project documentation

Confidentiality obligations do not apply to information that is publicly available or independently developed without use of the other party's confidential information.

Force Majeure
Neither party shall be liable for delays or failures in performance resulting from events beyond their reasonable control.
Governing Law
This agreement shall be governed by and construed in accordance with the laws of India, with exclusive jurisdiction resting in the courts of Chennai, Tamil Nadu.
Entire Agreement
This document constitutes the entire agreement between Lune Studio and the Client, superseding all prior communications, proposals, or understandings.
Third-Party Costs
Costs for external services required to run the website (including Sanity CMS, web hosting, domain registration, and premium font licenses) are not included in the project fee. The Client agrees to create accounts and provide billing details directly to these providers.
